ENTRANCE HALL


The entrance hall serves as the foyer of a repurposed industrial building, now transformed into a residential complex. Its strategic position near the city's main street makes it a prominent showcase. The foyer spans across three levels: street level, elevator level, and staircase level. Following this distinctive feature, the space has been divided into three portals crafted from stone, steel, and glass, with each portal situated on one of the corresponding levels.
